About

Built for dependable engineering work.

Sri Sakthi Industries is an engineering and fabrication company based in Pudupalayam, Tiruvannamalai Dt. Since 1995, we have supported customers with practical engineering, strong workmanship, timely execution and project-ready coordination from workshop preparation to site work.

We undertake all engineering works, heavy fabrication, large truss and godown shed works, boiler repair and new boiler related works, commercial kitchen equipment, machinery fabrication and design support.
